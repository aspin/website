I'm a developer, designer, and social scientist.

I'm also a student, TA, musician, mathematician, and engineer. I study Computer Science at Northwestern and conduct research with Delta Lab .

I code and play with almost any language. I do freelance and I'm well acquainted with multiple web and mobile platforms.
